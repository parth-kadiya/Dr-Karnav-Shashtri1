About

The Genesis of Karnavbhai

Born into a Brahmin family on the 7th of August 1991, Karnavbhai entered the world in the village of Kadi, nestled in the Gandhinagar district of Gujarat. His father, Shrideepakbhai Shashtri, serves as a government employee at the local education department secretariat, while his mother, Rakshaben, fulfills the role of a dedicated homemaker.

Early Life

Born in the village of Kadi, Karnavbhai hails from a dedicated family of government service and homemaking traditions.

Core Values

Strongly rooted in discipline and dedication, Karnavbhai's life reflects perseverance and integrity.

Vision

Aimed at societal betterment and community development, his vision focuses on education and empowerment.

The Enlightened Journey

Early Spiritual Awakening
